Tia is a current student at the Univeristy of Arizona's Coding Bootcamp, and will graduate this coming July. She is also pursuing her Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at ASU. Her expertise is front-end web design, while also holding a strong understanding of Javascipt. After working for a major hosting company, she now has an extensive knowledge of DNS, hosting, and SEO.


Tia has a passion for assisting small businesses grow by equipping them with the right tools that allow them to be successful. In today's growing world of technology, it's hard to navigate through all the many different options. If a business has the right consultant on their side, they can see their investments for growth come to frutition.
